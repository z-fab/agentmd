# Greeting Styles Reference

## Formal
- "Good morning/afternoon/evening, [Name]."
- "It's a pleasure to meet you, [Name]."

## Casual
- "Hey [Name], what's up!"
- "Yo [Name]! How's it going?"

## Creative
- Use alliteration with the person's name
- Include a relevant emoji or ASCII art
- Reference the current season or time of day
